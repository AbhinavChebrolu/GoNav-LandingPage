How we work

Your trusted partners

GoNav exists to help superyacht yacht captains, senior crew members, owners’ representatives, yacht brokers and estate managers take their guest events programmes to a whole new level.

Every GoNav event brings with it a new story. Each unique, bespoke adventure transports guests to a previously undiscovered or forgotten world, where they’re immersed in mystery, history, make-believe and discovery.

If you can dream it, we can deliver it.

Total support

Regardless of the scale of your events programme –� and however large, grand or complex your idea – we have the creative, logistical and technical skills to delight your guests in ways you never thought possible.

Together with our team of specialist events managers, professional actors, creative costume designers, make-up artists, set designers, videographers, graphic designers and more, we create fantastical worlds of discovery and magic that inspire and wow your guests.

Whether you like to be involved every step of the way or prefer to leave it to the experts, we’re a trusted pair of hands. We’ll guide you through the process – from the initial concept to the spectacular end result –� leaving nothing behind but footprints and memories.

Planning and preparation

GoNav events are a fine balance of visionary thinking, meticulous planning, careful preparation… and a healthy amount of flexibility.

Working closely with clients, we scout the best locations, liaise with local cultural experts, and oversee our incredible creative partners behind the scenes to maintain the magic and illusion, every minute of every event.

Talent and discretion

We’ve built GoNav by surrounding ourselves with incredible talent, drive and passion. We work with true experts, whose experience and skills are rare in the events world.

From specialist performers and creative craftsmen to authorities on local culture and history, every member of our team understands the exclusivity and privilege of the world we work in, and discretion comes as standard.
